  • Let's hear about it

Come on, make some NOISE.

TOO COOL MUSIC SCHOOL is your local leader for training in showmanship, musical theory and technique.

No other program in eastern Oklahoma brings out your talent for bringing the house down like we do. Our talented teachers offer classes on guitar, violin, banjo, piano, drums, and more. Book your first lesson today. We can't wait for you to hear the difference.

  • Find Out More

Theory

From rock-n-roll to country to jazz, you will build a solid foundation to prime you for success. We teach you to read sheet music and guitar tabs.

Technique

From strumming to drumming, you will have access to the latest in learning modes for quick success. We offer group classes and regular concerts to show off your skills.

Practice

Whether you're preparing for a showcase, an agent, or just need a little help along, our esteemed instructors are here to offer you the extra support that you need.

Strings

Guitar. Bass. Learn to pick, shred, strum, slide, wail.

Percussion

Drums. Traps. Everything you need to drop the beat.

Bandmates

Harmonize with your classmates and guest musicians.

Solo

Lead the group or brave a solo.
